Ingredients

Vegan, cruelty-free, and hand-crafted in the UK, our soaps and melts are made using the finest natural ingredients, all ethically sourced. Our luxurious range is made with pure essential oils to create uplifting and nurturing scents. Our Story

Melissa's Melts started in 2021 in Whitworth, Rochdale, as a small family business dedicated to crafting high-quality, handmade soaps and melts. Our mission is to provide luxurious, natural products made with the finest ingredients. We believe in sustainability and ethical sourcing, ensuring that our products are good for your skin and the planet. Join us in celebrating the art of natural soap making, crafted with love and care.
